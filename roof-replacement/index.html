Roof Replacement

Complete roof replacement with durable shingles, clean installation, and clear project communication.

A full roof replacement should solve the problem for years, not create new uncertainty. Royal Roofing Solutions helps homeowners choose the right roofing system, ventilation details, flashing approach, and installation schedule before work begins.

Replacement process

  1. Full roof inspection and condition report
  2. Written scope with material recommendations
  3. Scheduling, delivery coordination, and site preparation
  4. Tear-off, deck review, flashing, underlayment, and shingle installation
  5. Final cleanup, magnet sweep, and walkthrough

Built for long-term protection

The roof system is more than shingles. We look at ventilation, pipe boots, valleys, flashing, drip edge, decking, and water-shedding details so the finished installation protects the whole home.
