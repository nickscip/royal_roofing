FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much does a roof repair cost in Texas? ⌄

Roof repair cost depends on the damage, roof material, slope, access, and whether water has reached decking or interior areas. In Dallas, small repairs can be a few hundred dollars, while leak repairs commonly run higher when flashing, vents, shingles, decking, or interior protection are involved. Royal Roofing can inspect the issue and provide a clear repair estimate before work begins.

How quickly can you repair my roof? ⌄

Royal Roofing prioritizes active leaks, storm openings, and damage that could let water into the home. Timing depends on weather, safety, material availability, and the repair size, but we can inspect the problem, explain the next step, and recommend temporary protection when a permanent repair cannot be completed right away.

Should I repair or replace my roof? ⌄

A repair usually makes sense when the damage is local and the rest of the roof is still in good condition. Replacement is often the better value when the roof is near the end of its life, has widespread hail or wind damage, repeated leaks, failing decking, or repair costs that keep adding up. Royal Roofing can show you the problem areas and explain both options clearly.

Do you offer emergency roof repair? ⌄

Yes. Royal Roofing can help with urgent roof issues such as active leaks, missing shingles, storm damage, damaged flashing, and openings that need fast attention. When conditions are unsafe during severe weather, we focus first on protecting the property and then complete the repair once the roof can be worked on safely.

Will my insurance cover roof repair? ⌄

Insurance may cover sudden storm damage from hail, wind, or falling debris, but it usually does not cover normal wear, age, poor maintenance, or pre-existing problems. Royal Roofing can document visible damage with photos and provide a repair estimate, but your insurance carrier decides coverage. In Texas, roofing contractors cannot waive, absorb, or rebate your deductible.

How often should roof maintenance be performed? ⌄

For DFW homes, Royal Roofing recommends at least one roof check each year and after major hail, high wind, or heavy rain. Roofing industry guidance often recommends inspections in spring and fall, plus after severe storms. Regular maintenance helps catch loose shingles, failing sealant, clogged valleys, flashing issues, and small leaks before they become expensive repairs.
