FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do you provide framing services? ⌄

No. Royal Roofing does not provide framing services. For new construction, the builder or framing contractor should complete the roof framing first. Once the structure is framed, decked, and ready for roofing, Royal Roofing can inspect the deck, confirm roof details, and install the roofing system.

Do you work with builders and contractors? ⌄

Yes. Royal Roofing works with homeowners, builders, and contractors on new construction projects. We can review roof plans, coordinate material delivery, schedule installation around the build timeline, and communicate roof details such as flashing, ventilation, valleys, and penetrations.

What roofing materials do you offer? ⌄

Royal Roofing can help with common DFW new-build roofing options, including architectural asphalt shingles, Class 4 impact-resistant shingles, metal roofing, and TPO or other low-slope systems where the design calls for it. We will help match the material to the roof slope, budget, building use, style, and North Texas weather needs.

Can you install both commercial and residential new builds? ⌄

Yes. Royal Roofing can install roofing systems for residential new builds and commercial new-build projects when the scope, access, materials, and schedule are a good fit. Residential projects often use shingles or metal, while commercial projects may call for TPO, metal panels, or another system based on the plans.

Do you provide free estimates for new builds? ⌄

Yes. Royal Roofing can provide a free estimate for new construction roofing. For the most accurate quote, we will need the project location, roof plans or measurements, material choice, roof slope, roof complexity, ventilation details, and the expected build schedule.
