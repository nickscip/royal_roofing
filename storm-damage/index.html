Storm Damage

Roof inspections and repair support after hail, wind, heavy rain, and severe weather.

After severe weather, small roof issues can turn into expensive interior damage. Royal Roofing Solutions checks for missing shingles, lifted tabs, hail impact marks, exposed fasteners, damaged flashing, and gutter problems.

When to schedule an inspection

  • Shingles are missing, creased, or loose
  • Granules are collecting near downspouts
  • Water spots appear on ceilings or walls
  • Gutters, vents, or flashing look dented
  • Neighbors are getting roofs inspected after the same storm

Insurance support

We can document visible damage, explain the repair scope, and coordinate with the homeowner during the claim process. The goal is simple: clear information and repair work that matches the actual condition of the roof.
