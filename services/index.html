ROOFING TYPES

SHINGLES

  • Best value for most homes
  • Typically lasts 20-30 years
  • Easy to repair and maintain
  • Many colors and style options

TPO

  • Great value for flat roofs
  • Can last 20-30 years
  • Low-maintenance surface
  • Reflects heat and resists leaks

PROPANEL

  • Higher upfront, long-term value
  • Can last 40+ years
  • Very low maintenance
  • Durable with a clean metal look
