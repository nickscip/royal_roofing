FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What types of commercial roofing systems does Royal Roofing install? ⌄

Royal Roofing can help with common DFW commercial roofing systems, including TPO and other low-slope membrane systems, metal roofing, modified bitumen, and commercial shingle applications where the building design calls for it. We will recommend the system based on roof slope, drainage, building use, budget, and North Texas heat, hail, and wind exposure.

How much does commercial roofing cost in Texas? ⌄

Commercial roofing in Texas is usually priced by the square foot, and many projects fall somewhere around $4 to $23 per square foot depending on the system, insulation, tear-off, roof access, drainage, penetrations, and hidden deck damage. Royal Roofing can provide a written estimate after reviewing the property and project scope.

How do you minimize business disruption during commercial roofing? ⌄

Royal Roofing can plan work around business operations whenever possible. That may include coordinating access areas, material delivery, staging, safety zones, noise expectations, daily cleanup, and communication with the property owner or manager before work begins.

Do you offer commercial roofing maintenance? ⌄

Yes. Royal Roofing can inspect commercial roofs and help identify maintenance needs such as open seams, punctures, ponding water, clogged drains, loose flashing, failing sealant, and storm damage. In DFW, regular inspections are especially helpful after hail, high wind, and heavy rain.

Does Royal Roofing handle commercial storm damages claims? ⌄

Royal Roofing can inspect storm damage, document visible roof conditions, take photos, and provide a repair or replacement estimate. In Texas, a roofing contractor cannot act as a public insurance adjuster on the same project, so the insurance company and any licensed adjuster make claim coverage decisions.

What roofing permits does Royal Roofing handle? ⌄

Permit requirements vary by city across Dallas-Fort Worth. In Dallas, commercial permits are used for construction, alteration, and renovation work to help confirm code, zoning, and safety compliance. Royal Roofing can help identify the permit steps needed for your commercial roofing project and include them in the project plan.
