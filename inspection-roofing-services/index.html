FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much does a roofing inspection cost in Texas? ⌄

Royal Roofing can let you know up front whether your inspection has a cost based on the reason for the visit and the level of documentation needed. In the DFW area, paid residential roof inspections commonly range from about $150 to $400 depending on roof size, slope, access, and report detail.

How long does a roof inspection take? ⌄

Most standard residential roof inspections take about 30 to 90 minutes on site. Larger roofs, steep slopes, attic checks, storm damage documentation, drone photos, or commercial roofs can take longer. Royal Roofing will explain what we are checking before the inspection begins.

Do I need a roof inspection before selling my home? ⌄

It is a smart step in DFW, especially after hail or high-wind seasons. Texas sellers are commonly expected to disclose known roof defects and prior roof repairs, and a roof inspection can help you understand the condition before listing, negotiating, or responding to a buyer's inspection.

How often should I get my roof inspected? ⌄

Royal Roofing recommends an inspection at least once a year and after major hail, wind, or heavy rain events in North Texas. Roofing industry guidance often recommends checking roofs in spring and fall, plus after severe storms, so small issues can be found before they become expensive repairs.
